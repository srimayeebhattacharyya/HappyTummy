HappyTummy Foundation is a food-redistribution initiative inspired by organizations like FoodBox Foundation. We help restaurants, caterers, and home kitchens donate their extra, safe-to-eat food to verified NGOs, shelters, and community groups ‚Äî quickly and transparently.
